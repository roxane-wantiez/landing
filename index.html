Our history

Founded in 2021 by a group of Essec alumni, our patisserie learning platform was created in collaboration with many renowned patissiers including Cyril Lignac and many others. The objective of this initiative is to give everyone the opportunity to learn pastry in a simple and educational way

How to proceed?

No matter what your level of knowledge and practice in pastry. You can register simply by indicating your level, your ambitions and your preferences. The application is open to beginners as well as the more experienced!
